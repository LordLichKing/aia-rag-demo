# Compliance Guide / 合规指南

## 1. Data Protection Policy / 数据保护政策

### 1.1 General Principles / 一般原则

All employees must comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations, including but not limited to:
- Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL) of China
- General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable
- Company internal data protection policies

所有员工必须遵守适用的数据保护法律法规，包括但不限于《个人信息保护法》(PIPL) 和适用的《通用数据保护条例》(GDPR)。

### 1.2 Data Classification / 数据分类

Company data is classified into four levels:
1. **Public (公开)**: Information that can be freely shared
2. **Internal (内部)**: For internal use only
3. **Confidential (机密)**: Restricted access, requires authorization
4. **Top Secret (绝密)**: Highest level, extremely limited access

### 1.3 Data Handling Requirements / 数据处理要求

- Personal data must be collected only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes
- Data minimization: collect only what is necessary
- Retention periods must be defined and enforced
- Cross-border data transfers require additional approvals

## 2. Security Incident Response / 安全事件响应

### 2.1 Reporting / 报告

Security incidents must be reported immediately to the IT Security team:
- Email: security@company.com
- Hotline: +86-xxx-xxxx-xxxx
- Emergency: Contact your direct supervisor

安全事件必须立即报告给IT安全团队。

### 2.2 Response Procedure / 响应流程

1. **Identify**: Determine the nature and scope of the incident
2. **Contain**: Take immediate steps to prevent further damage
3. **Assess**: Evaluate the impact and severity
4. **Notify**: Inform relevant stakeholders and authorities as required
5. **Recover**: Restore affected systems and data
6. **Review**: Conduct post-incident review and update procedures

## 3. Code of Conduct / 行为准则

### 3.1 Anti-Corruption / 反腐败

The company maintains a zero-tolerance policy towards corruption and bribery. All business dealings must be conducted transparently and ethically.

### 3.2 Conflict of Interest / 利益冲突

Employees must disclose any potential conflicts of interest to their supervisor and the compliance department.

### 3.3 Whistleblower Protection / 举报人保护

The company protects employees who report violations in good faith. Reports can be made anonymously through the compliance hotline.
